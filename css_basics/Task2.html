Rich History

Agra, home to the world-famous Taj Mahal, has been a symbol of India’s rich Mughal heritage. It was once the capital of the Mughal Empire and holds architectural wonders that tell stories of love and power.

Famous Places

From the majestic Taj Mahal to Agra Fort and Fatehpur Sikri, the city showcases some of the finest Mughal architecture ever built. Each monument offers a glimpse into history and artistic brilliance.

Vibrant Culture

The city is known for its handicrafts, marble work, and delicious Petha sweets. Festivals like Diwali and Holi are celebrated with unmatched enthusiasm and color.
